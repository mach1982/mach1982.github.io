Dobby the Fee Elf.

Introduction

Many of you may have hear from you Wizard friends or family of the exploits of Harry Potter the Boy who lived , but you may have not heard of Dobby the house Elf.

Freed by Harry Pooter at the end of his Second year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry by tricking his cruel master and Death Eater Lucius Malfoy into give Dobby a sock and therefore giving him clothes and freeing him.

As a sign of his gratitude Dobby became as loyal to Harry Potter as any member of the infamous Dumbledore's Army and help Harry' out on a number of occasions. This unwavering loyalty to the Wizard that had freed him, ultimately ended in him give his life to save Harry and his companions and the prisons of He who Must No Be Name.

Though Dobby was only free for a for a couple of years this left huge impact on the Wizardry Society, to this day when a Wizard or Witich intervenes to save a Muggles life it called “Doing a Dobby”. In his memory a new Position of "Free Elf" , basically a Elf liaison officer , was created in the Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures. This is a paid position for any Elf . The current holder is Dobby's good friend Winky..
